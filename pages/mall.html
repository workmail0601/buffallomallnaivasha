Who We Are

Buffalo Mall Naivasha stands as a testament to the growth and vibrancy of the Nakuru County region. Strategically located along the Nairobi-Nakuru Highway, our mall serves as the premier retail hub for residents and visitors exploring the Great Rift Valley.

With curated retail spaces, we provide an international shopping experience that honors Kenyan hospitality. From daily essentials to high-fashion and gourmet dining, every corner of our mall is designed with the visitor's comfort in mind.

Getting Here

Located directly off the A104 Nairobi-Nakuru Highway. We are approximately 5 minutes from Naivasha town center, making us the perfect stop for travelers and locals alike.
